    MineFuse

  • About MineFuse:

    ● Terrorists start with a bomb
    ● Terrorists have to plant the bomb at either of the two bomb sites (A, or B)
    ● The bomb takes 45 seconds to detonate.
    ● Counter-terrorists have to kill the terrorists, and defuse the bomb (if planted)
    ● When you die, you are "out" for the rest of the round.
    ● If the bomb blows up, terrorists win, If the bomb is defused, counter-terrorists win.
    ● If the other team is all killed, your team wins.
    ● The game is won in a fashion that is best of 30 rounds, with the 15 rounds mark as the team switch
    ● Compass that points to the bomb.
    ● Maps over a bungeecord network, to manage server resources and distribute players around.
    ● Scoreboard information.
    ● Tab menu information. Stats
    ● CrankShot Weapons
